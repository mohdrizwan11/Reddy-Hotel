

# Reddy Hotel — Minimalistic Restaurant Website

## Overview
A clean, single-page website for Reddy Hotel featuring your logo, menu, contact details, and **WhatsApp ordering**. Warm maroon/burgundy color scheme inspired by your logo.

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Hero Section
- Your Reddy Hotel logo prominently displayed
- Tagline: "Delicious food, served with love"
- "View Menu" and "Order on WhatsApp" call-to-action buttons

### 2. Menu Section
- Categorized menu cards for **Rice Dishes**, **Starters**, and **Noodles**
- Each item showing name and price (₹)
- Each menu item gets an **"Order Now"** button that opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message like: *"Hi, I'd like to order [Item Name] (₹Price) from Reddy Hotel"*
- "Online Delivery Available" badge/banner

### 3. WhatsApp Ordering
- A floating WhatsApp button (green, bottom-right corner) visible on all sections for quick access
- Tapping it opens WhatsApp chat with the number **9100284142** and a greeting message
- The menu "Order Now" buttons will also link to WhatsApp with the specific item pre-filled

### 4. Contact Section
- Owner: Mallela Mahesh Reddy
- Click-to-call links for both phone numbers (9100284142 & 9182870092)
- WhatsApp chat links for both numbers
- Click-to-email link
- Address with Google Maps placeholder

### 5. Footer
- "Thank you for choosing Reddy Hotel!"
- Quick links to menu and contact

## Design Style
- **Minimalistic** with lots of white space
- **Color palette**: Maroon/burgundy (from logo) + cream/off-white background + green WhatsApp accent
- Mobile-responsive single-page layout
- Smooth scroll navigation
